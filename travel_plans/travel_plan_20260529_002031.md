## 🗓️ Itinerary
Given the constraints, I'll plan a complete trip from Patna to Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam, including flights, itinerary, hotel bookings, and everything.

**Flight Booking:**
Since there are no direct flights from Patna to Ho Chi Minh City, I recommend flying from Patna to Delhi and then taking a connecting flight from Delhi to Ho Chi Minh City. Here's a suggested flight itinerary:

* Patna (PAT) to Delhi (DEL) on IndiGo or SpiceJet
* Delhi (DEL) to Ho Chi Minh City (SGN) on Vietnam Airlines, IndiGo, or Singapore Airlines

You can book your flights on Expedia, Orbitz, or directly on the airlines' websites.

**Itinerary:**
For an 8-10 day trip to Vietnam, I've created a suggested itinerary:

Day 1: Arrival in Ho Chi Minh City

* Arrive at Tan Son Nhat International Airport (SGN)
* Check-in at La Vela Saigon Hotel or similar
* Explore the city, visit the War Remnants Museum, and try some street food

Day 2: Ho Chi Minh City

* Visit the Cu Chi Tunnels, a network of underground tunnels used during the Vietnam War
* Explore the Notre Dame Cathedral, Central Post Office, and Ben Thanh Market
* Enjoy a traditional Vietnamese dinner and watch a water puppet show

Day 3: Ho Chi Minh City to Hanoi

* Take a morning flight from Ho Chi Minh City to Hanoi (approximately 2 hours)
* Check-in at a hotel in the Old Quarter, such as the Hanoi Holiday Diamond Hotel
* Explore the Old Quarter, visit the Dong Xuan Market, and try some local street food

Day 4: Hanoi

* Visit the Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum, the Presidential Palace, and the One Pillar Pagoda
* Explore the Temple of Literature, a historic university and temple complex
* Enjoy a traditional Vietnamese lunch and take a cyclo ride through the Old Quarter

Day 5: Hanoi to Ha Long Bay

* Take a day trip to Ha Long Bay, a stunning natural wonder with over 1,600 limestone islands and islets
* Cruise through the bay, visit caves, and enjoy a seafood lunch on board
* Return to Hanoi in the evening and enjoy a traditional Vietnamese dinner

Day 6: Hanoi to Da Nang

* Take a morning flight from Hanoi to Da Nang (approximately 1.5 hours)
* Check-in at the Hyatt Regency Danang Resort and Spa or similar
* Explore the city, visit the Marble Mountains, and enjoy the beach

Day 7: Da Nang

* Visit the ancient town of Hoi An, a UNESCO World Heritage Site
* Explore the Japanese Bridge, the Phuc Kien Assembly Hall, and the local markets
* Enjoy a traditional Vietnamese lunch and take a cooking class to learn how to make local dishes

Day 8: Da Nang to Ho Chi Minh City

* Take a morning flight from Da Nang to Ho Chi Minh City (approximately 1.5 hours)
* Spend the day shopping for souvenirs, visiting any last-minute attractions, and enjoying a farewell dinner

Day 9-10: Departure from Ho Chi Minh City

* Spend the morning at leisure, relaxing at the hotel or exploring the city
* Depart for the airport and fly back to Patna via Delhi
